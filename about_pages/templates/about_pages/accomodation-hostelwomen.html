HOSTELS FOR WOMEN


There are 7 blocks allotted for Women students of capacity varying from 250 to 700 in each block. The total capacity for Women’s hostel is about 4000.


  • Floor Plans

Sister Nivedita (M Block)

Welcome to Sister Nivedita hostel, also known as M-Block, is a fully furnished Girls’ hostel. This residential facility offers a comfortable accommodation to more than 1800 girls every year. It has 24 X 7 CCTV coverage, security guards surveillance, power backup and biometric attendance system which ensure the utmost security for girls.

Other amenities like Wi-Fi, fully equipped gym, indoor games hall, jogging and walking tracks, fully air-conditioned waiting halls, study hall ensure the utmost comfort for the students. Supermarkets, eatery shops, photocopier service and all the other shops catering to the daily requirement of the students are also present inside the premises.

This block’s entrance is facing north and all the rooms are fully furnished with attached bathrooms with cold and hot water facilities. In each room Study table, storage lockers, are available on double sharing basis.

Both A.C and Non A.C rooms are available for allotment. From First floor to Eighth floor both AC and Non-AC Rooms are available as mentioned in the floor plan. From 9th floor to 15th floor all the rooms are Non-AC.

Senbagam Block

Senbagam girls Hostel has Ground plus 5 floors. This fully furnished hostel provides a comfortable stay, with a safe and a conducive environment for the girl students. For the safety of girls, it is equipped with 24 * 7 surveillance cameras, security guards and biometric attendance.

Thishostel provides WiFi, power backup and purified water facility. Students can spend their leisure time in the TV hall. Eatery shops, Photocopier service are also available near the block.

Senbagam block is a north facing building, with a dining hall in the ground floor. Rooms are furnished with a bed, wardrobe, study table, and chair. The rooms are spacious and are available on triple sharing and six sharing basis. Common bathrooms with cold and hot water facility are available. The block can house above 400 students.

ESQ A BLOCK

Welcome to Engineering Staff Quarters A, also known as ESQ-A block, is a well furnished Girls hostel. This residential facility offers a homely accommodation, like living in a flat with balconies. This block has 30 flats (G + 9) and 2 lifts. Medical clinic room is there very nearby. Each flat is available on two, three and four sharing basis with a study hall. All the flats are Non AC and some of the flats are provided with attached washrooms.

Block entrance is facing east and all rooms are provided with study tables and storage lockers. Cold and hot water facilities, 24 x 7 Wi-fi facilities, indoor and outdoor games, jogging and walking tracks are also available. Eatery shops, photocopier service are present near the premises. It has security guards surveillance, power backup and biometric attendance system which ensure the utmost security for girls.

ESQ B BLOCK

Welcome to Engineering Staff Quarters B, also known as ESQ-B block, is a well furnished Girls hostel. This residential facility offers a homely accommodation, like living in a flat with balconies. This block has 40 flats (G + 9) and 2 lifts. Clinic room is available to ensure the medical facilities for the students. Each flat is available on two and three sharing basis with a study hall. All the flats are Non AC and some of the flats are provided with attached washrooms.

Block entrance is facing east and all rooms are provided with study tables and storage lockers. Cold and hot water facilities, 24 x 7 Wi-fi facilities, indoor and outdoor games, jogging and walking tracks are also available. Eatery shops, photocopier service are also present near the premises. It has security guards surveillance, power backup and biometric attendance system which ensure the utmost security for girls.

Kalpana Chawla Hostel

Welcome to Kalpana Chawla hostel. It is a fully furnished Girls’ hostel, which has rooms from 2nd to 5th floor with a lift. Each floor has 25 AC rooms. This residential facility offers a comfortable accommodation to more than 250 girls every year. It has 24 X 7 CCTV coverage, security guards surveillance, power backup and biometric attendance system.

Other amenities like Wi-Fi, indoor games, TV hall, study hall ensure the utmost comfort for the students.

Supermarkets, eatery shops, photocopier service and all the other shops catering to the daily requirement of the students are also present very near to the premises. This block’s entrance is facing west and all the rooms are fully furnished with attached bathrooms with cold and hot water facilities. In each room Study table, storage lockers, are available on double sharing and triple sharing-basis.

Meenakshi Hostel

Welcome to Meenakshi hostel. It is a fully furnished Girls’ hostel, which has rooms from ground floor to fourth floor. Each floor has 20 to 30 non-AC rooms with attached wash rooms which are available on triple sharing basis. This residential facility offers a comfortable accommodation to more than 350 girls every year. It has 24 X 7 CCTV coverage, security guards surveillance, power backup and biometric attendance system.

Other amenities like Wi-Fi, indoor games, Cloth Washing area, Gym Hall. TV hall, study hall and mess hall ensure the utmost comfort for the students. Supermarkets, eatery shops, photocopier service and all the other shops catering to the daily requirement of the students are also present very near to the premises. This block’s entrance is facing east and all the rooms are fully furnished with attached bathrooms with cold and hot water facilities. In each room Study table, storage lockers, ceiling fan are available.
