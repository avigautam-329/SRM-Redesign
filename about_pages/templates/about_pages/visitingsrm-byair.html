Visiting SRM > By Air

BY AIR

The nearest railway station from the Chennai Airport is Thrisulam, located just opposite the Airport. Get on a train towards Tambaram and follow the instructions given in the 'By Rail' section above for reaching SRM Institute of Science and Technology (formerly known as SRM University).

Shuttle bus service is available from SRM Institute of Science and Technology (formerly known as SRM University) main gate to commute around the university.
