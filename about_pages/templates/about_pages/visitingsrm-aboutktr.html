KATTANKULATHUR LAYOUT

The SRM Institute of Science and Technology (formerly known as SRM University) Main Campus (Kattankulathur) is 40 kms from Chennai city on NH 45, and around 20 kms from Chennai Airport. The nearest Bus stop and Railway station is Potheri. The University is well connected by road, rail and air.

The Layout of the Kattankulathur Campus is as follows:

This main campus is at Kattankulathur in Chengalpattu District, a suburb of Chennai City on the G.S.T. Road (Chennai - Trichy National Highway, NH 45), 20 kms from Chennai Airport.

This 250 acre campus houses all the four faculties including:

  • Engineering & Technology (erstwhile SRM Engineering College).
  • Medical & Health Sciences (Medical College and Research Center, Dental College, College of Pharmacy, College of Nursing, College of Physiotherapy, College of Occupational therapy and School of Public Health).
  • School of Management.
  • Faculty of Science & Humanities (journalism, communication, biotechnology and education). This campus has a network of 30 blocks for academic and administrative activities with a built up area of over 1,50,000 sq m.

Other institutes and facilities in the Kattankulathur campus are:

  • Valliammai Engineering College
  • Valliammai Polytechnic
  • SRM Polytechnic
  • SRM Institute of Hotel Management
  • SRM Arts and Science College
  • The SRM Hotel
  • General Hospital
