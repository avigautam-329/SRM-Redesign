INTERNATIONAL ADVISORY BOARD

SRM Institute of Science and Technology (formerly known as SRM University) is one of the first universities in India to constitute an International Advisory Board (IAB) in 2003 with 31 Distinguished Deans, Directors and professors from top universities across the world. The membership has grown to over 50 today.The IAB is actively involved in building a stronger international dimension - in curriculum, research and teaching methodology. It has helped to create innovative avenues for research and successful careers at SRM Institute of Science and Technology (formerly known as SRM University), by:

  • Advising on implementation of systems and processes to take the standard of higher education to international standards
  • Identifying and facilitating cooperation with reputed universities and industries in their country, for collaborative academic and research programs
  • Formulating research schemes for availing external funding both in India and abroad
