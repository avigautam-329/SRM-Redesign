PANEL OF FOREIGN FACULTY

FOREIGN FACULTY ON LONG TENURE

Foreign visiting faculty are a boon to the university as they help

  • Promote international education.
  • Enhance the quality of research.
  • Build exposure to different cultures and international trends

At present, nearly 10 foreign faculty are teaching at SRM Institute of Science and Technology (formerly known as SRM University) on long tenure. Many more visit the campus for academic and research purposes on an ongoing basis. This gives both the students and the faculty greater opportunity for mutual interaction and learning. Among the current list of foreign faculty at SRM are:

Dr.Nikolai Khartchenko, Germany Prof.Hugo Sandanter, Columbia Ms.Clotilde Varennes, France Mr.Aurelien Le Poupon, France Ms.Gwenaelle Raisoir, France Ms.Sukhee Lee, Korea Dr. Thimmiah Muralidharam, USA Dr. Park, Korea Dr. Mohan Ranganathan, France

Over the next three years, SRM university plans to increase the Indian faculty to international visiting faculty ratio to 60:40.
