WHO CAN APPLY?


Eligible Categories:

  • Full time bonafide research scholars (M. Phil. & Ph.D. students).
  • Full time bonafide undergraduate & postgraduate students of Faculty of Engineering and Technology, Medicine and Health Sciences, Science & Humanities.
  • Full time bonafide under graduate & post graduate students of SRM Family of Educational Institution.
  • Full-time bonafide diploma students of Polytechnic and Hotel Management.

Non - Eligible Categories:

  • Working men / women and ex-students.
  • Students who have failed and are not re-admitted as regular students.
  • Students enrolled in an evening course, correspondence or any external course of study.
  • Students penalized for indiscipline activities.
