CONSTITUTION OF INTERNAL COMPLAINTS COMMITTEE

In reference to UGC, Ministry of Human Resource Development, Government of India’s letters of even number dated 28.01.2015 and 14th May, 2019, SRM Institute of Science and Technology reconstituted the Internal Complaint Committee (ICC) with the following members to deal with the issue of gender based violence and to conduct gender sensitization programme.

To contact the committee:

  • E-mail - po.icc.ktr@srmist.edu.in
  • Phone – 9677760276 / 7358026017

The above constituted ICC will be working as per UGC (Prevention, Prohibition and Redressal of Sexual Harassment of Women Employees and Students in Higher Educational Institutions) Regulation, 2015.
